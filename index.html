About me

My name is Matt Pollicito, I am a very technology-based individual! I was born in Hartford, CT during November 1989. I have one sibling, an older brother, and ZERO first cousins (wild, I know).
I have always been passionate about creativity and expression of one's self. I enjoyed drawing, painting and coloring since childhood, these interests are what got me where I am today!
During my high school years, I began taking graphic design classes to start my journey. I attended Tunxis CC part-time after high school to lay groundwork for my future.
